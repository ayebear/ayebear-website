Notation

l, r = Inner slice move
Lw, Rw = Multiple layers
L, R = Outer layers



4x4x4

PLL parity:
r2 U2 r2 Uw2 r2 u2

Alternate PLL parity:
Uw2 Rw2 U2 r2 U2 Rw2 Uw2

OLL parity:
Rw U2 Rw x U2 r U2 x' l' U2 l U2 r' U2 r U2 Rw' U2 Rw'



5x5x5

Swap two edges on UF face:
Rw2 B2 U2 Lw U2 Rw' U2 Rw U2 F2 Rw F2 Lw' B2 Rw2

Swap two edges between UF and UB:
Lw' U2 Lw' U2 F2 Lw' F2 Rw U2 Rw' U2 Lw2
